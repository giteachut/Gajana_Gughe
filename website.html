About

Gajanan Vitthalrao Ghuge


Vice-President BJP Maharashtra
Ex-MLA Kalamnuri Assembly


Gajanan Vitthalrao Ghuge, an influential figure in Maharashtra's political landscape, was born on 22 August, 1963, in Kalamnuri. His political journey, which commenced in 1991, has been marked by significant milestones. Notably, he secured victory in the 1999 Maharashtra Legislative Assembly elections, earning the position of MLA for Kalamnuri, and again in 2004 to 2009.

Ghuge's impact extends across various sectors, including agriculture, irrigation, education, health, and social welfare. He has spearheaded multiple development projects in his constituency, emphasizing a commitment to grassroots development. A vocal advocate for farmers' rights, reservation, water scarcity, corruption, and communal harmony, Ghuge currently holds the position of state vice-president in the BJP OBC Morcha and serves as the president of the Maharashtra State Cooperative Sugar Factories Federation.

In the digital age, Ghuge remains connected with the public through active engagement on social media platforms. His updates, live sessions, and interactive approach reflect a commitment to transparent and accessible leadership, fostering a strong connection with the people he serves.

Social Works

"Cow Conservation Campaign"

Gajanan Ghuge actively engages in the "Adopt-a-Cow Conservation Campaign," emphasizing the crucial role of cows in agriculture and the environment. Encouraging individuals to adopt both cows and their calves, Ghuge promotes responsible and humane treatment of these animals. His initiative reflects a deep commitment to environmental sustainability and ethical animal husbandry, fostering awareness and positive community impact. Ghuge's leadership in the campaign exemplifies his dedication to holistic conservation practices, addressing the interconnected issues of environmental and animal welfare in a meaningful and impactful way.

"Stationery for Hope"

Gajanan Ghuge, a compassionate and dedicated politician, has initiated a heartwarming project to celebrate the birthdays with needy rural students. Understanding the financial constraints faced by these families, Ghuge distributes special stationery kits on their birthday, combining the joy of celebration with essential educational support. These thoughtful gifts not only bring smiles to the faces of the students but also contribute significantly to their academic journeys... Ghuge's innovative approach reflects his commitment to holistic community development, emphasizing the transformative power of education. Through initiatives like these, he continues to set an inspiring example of compassionate leadership, making a meaningful impact on the lives of the most vulnerable members of the community.

"Clean India Initiative"

Gajanan Ghuge, a dedicated politician, has recently spearheaded a noteworthy initiative in the form of the Clean India campaign. Recognizing the critical need for a cleaner and healthier environment, Ghuge has actively engaged in organizing and participating in cleanliness drives across various localities. By mobilizing community members and volunteers..., he has worked towards creating awareness about the importance of maintaining cleanliness in public spaces. This initiative reflects Ghuge's commitment to fostering a sense of civic responsibility and contributing to the overall well-being of the community. Through these efforts, he aims to instill a lasting impact on the cleanliness and hygiene standards of the areas he serves.
